# Information
XyloLibrary is a non-profit organisation which allows people to learn without paying anything!
This open-source platform has articles which you can read to learn!
You can even teach people through your own knowledge by adding articles!

# Add your own article
1. Write your article.
2. Give a proper title and author name.
3. Submit by mailing at Xylo Tech Labs for checking by moderators.
4. After checking, if your article passes the test, it will be added to XyloLibrary, else it will be rejected and you will get a email about the reasons why it got rejected.

Note: The article should not contain any copyrighted material.
